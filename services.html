Gel Manicures and Extensions

The Gel Manicure And Extension Treatment Is Rising In Popularity. The Extension Application
Has Become Popular Due To Its Polish-like Appearance, It's A Different Feel From Acrylic And
Minimum Damage To The Nail Beds. These Extensions Have Also Become A Hit With Celebrities
And Influencers And Have Taken The Nail Spa Industry By Storm. Gel Manicures Are
Also Popular Because Of Their Durability.

Soak-Off Gel Manicure

  • In This Type Of Manicure, The Nail Lacquer Is Applied And Hardened Under The Ultraviolet Rays At Every Layer Applied With A Brush.


Paraffin Wax Manicure

  • A Method Which Involves Dipping Hands In Wax For Several Layers, Then Peeling It Off Along With Other Products Resulting In Soft, Moisturised Hands.

Brazilian Manicure

  • In This Type Of Manicure, The Entire Cuticle Is Removed And Then Nail Lacquer Is Applied


Franch Manicure

  • Similar To A Regular Manicure, Here The Manicurist Applies Clear & White Polish That Will Enhance The Look Of Natural Nails.

Spa Manicure

  • This Manicure Involves A Longer Treatment Of Exfoliation And Massage And Is A Relaxing Treatment For The Whole Hand.
